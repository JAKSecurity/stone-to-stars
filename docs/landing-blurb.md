## Game-page header (wraps the embed)

### Rogue·Civ
**Lead a civilization from the Stone Age to the stars — one desperate survivor run at a time.**

Rogue·Civ fuses a survivor-style auto-battler with a Civ-style tech tree. Drop into the wilds, dodge
the swarm with WASD while your weapons fire on their own, and haul gems home to fund the technology
that carries your people across nine ages — all the way to Space — and into The Last Stand.

**Controls** — Move: WASD · Attack: automatic · Active item: right-click · Pause: Esc

A tech demo built with heavy AI assistance. Source on
[GitHub](https://github.com/JAKSecurity/rogue-civ).
